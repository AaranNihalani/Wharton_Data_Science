WHSDSC 2026 – Phase 1 Crib Sheet

Model: Line-aware Elo / Bradley–Terry
Goal: Rank teams, predict matchups, quantify line depth, visualize insight



0. Setup (Day 1)

Data

• whl_2025 (game × line rows)

• WHSDSC_Rnd1_matchups

Key fields

• team, opponent, home/away

• offensive line (1st / 2nd)

• defensive pairing (1st / 2nd)

• xG, goals, time on ice

Assumption

• Team & line quality constant over season



1. Phase 1a — Team Strength & Matchups (Days 2–3)

Clean & Aggregate

• Aggregate line rows → team–game

• Compute:

◦ Total xG for / against

◦ xG per 60 (optional)

◦ Home indicator

Model (Elo-style)

Bradley–Terry logistic

P(home win)=σ(αhome−αaway+β⋅home)P(\text{home win})=\sigma(\alpha_{home}-\alpha_{away}+\beta\cdot home)P(home win)=σ(αhome−αaway+β⋅home)

• αi\alpha_iαi: team strength (Elo-like)

• β\betaβ: home advantage

Outputs

• Power rankings: rank teams by αi\alpha_iαi

• Matchups: compute 16 home win probabilities



2. Phase 1b — Offensive Line Disparity (Day 4)

Line Performance

For each team & line:

Line xG/60=∑xG∑TOI×60\text{Line xG/60}=\frac{\sum xG}{\sum TOI}\times 60Line xG/60=∑TOI∑xG×60

Disparity Ratio

Disparity=1st line xG/602nd line xG/60\text{Disparity}=\frac{\text{1st line xG/60}}{\text{2nd line xG/60}}Disparity=2nd line xG/601st line xG/60

Output

• Rank teams by disparity

• Submit Top 10 highest ratios
